Organisation Overview

Organisation Name: Afro-Facial Skincare
Website published on: https://github.com/Andiswa0311/ST10483385

Organisation history:
Afro-Facial Skincare is a skincare service brand that focuses on providing specialised facial treatments designed for melanin-rich and African textured skin. The business aims to address common skin concerns, such as hyperpigmentation, acne, dryness, and uneven skin tone, through professional facial treatments and personalized skincare consultations. The brand also plans to offer professional beauty workspaces for skincare specialists and beauty therapists. 


Mission Statement:
Afro-facial Skincare's main mission is to provide high-quality skincare treatments tailored specifically for melanin-rich skin so that African people can feel confident in their beautiful melanated skin while also empowering beauty professionals through access to professional workspace facilities.


Vision statement:
To become a trusted skincare and beauty studio known for improving skin health and supporting beauty professionals in the community.


Target Audiences:
1.	Individuals with melanin-rich skin seeking facial treatments
2.	Young adults and professionals concerned with skincare
3.	Beauty professionals looking for workspace rental
4.	Clients interested in personalised skincare consultations

Website Goals and Objectives

The Afro-Facial Skincare website aims to promote facial products and services, attract customers, and support business growth.

Website goals:
•	Promote Facial beauty products
•	Promote Afro Facial Skincare services
•	Allow customers to book facial treatments online
•	Provide information about skincare for melanin-rich skin
•	Advertise workspace rentals for beauty professionals


Key performance indicators KPIs:

•	Number of website visitors per month
•	Number of online bookings
•	Positive testimonials from customers
•	Workspace rental requests

Current Website Analysis

Currently, the Afro-Facial Skincare brand does not have an existing
website. 
However, creating a website will allow the business to establish an online presence, reach more customers, and provide convenient online booking.

Areas for improvement:
Since Afro-Facial Skincare does not have a current website, the main     
focus will be on developing a website that clearly presents the brand 
and its products and services.


Proposed Website Features and Functionality
 
Homepage

The homepage will introduce Afro-Facial Skincare and provide a brief overview 
of the business. It will highlight the brand’s focus on skincare treatments 
designed for melanated skin and provide easy navigation to other pages on 
website.

Services Page

The services page will list the facial treatments offered by Afro Facial Skincare. 
These services include:

•	Acne control facial
•	Skincare consultation
•	Appointment bookings
•	Dark Spot removal treatments

Images/Gallery Page
 
The gallery page will display images related to the business. These images
Include:

•	Skincare products
•	Beauty work space areas
•	Face models
•	Client skin before and after results

 
About Us Page

The About Us page will provide information about the business, including its 
mission and vision. It will explain the purpose of Afro Facial Skincare and its 
commitment to providing specialised facial treatments.

Contact Page

The contact page will provide important business information such as the 
phone number, email address, and location of Afro Facial Skincare. A contact 
form may also be included so customers can send inquiries directly through 
the website.
